# Design Refinement Summary

## Premium B2B Consulting Site — Finalized

This document outlines the strategic simplifications made to transform the site into an elite, credible B2B consulting presence for Solid Gear Designs.

---

## Strategic Removals

### 1. CTA Consolidation
**Before:** Multiple CTAs scattered throughout the site
- Hero: 2 buttons ("Book a Call" + "See Results")
- Services section: Additional "Book a Call" button
- Navigation: Sticky CTA button

**After:** Single, strategic CTA placement
- Hero: ONE "Book a Call" button only
- Contact section: Form submission button
- Navigation: Clean link structure, NO sticky CTA

**Reasoning:** Premium consultancies don't need aggressive conversion tactics. Confidence comes from restraint.

### Calm, Confident, Senior-Level
- No visual clutter
- No salesy conversion tricks

### Premium B2B Aesthetic
- Think McKinsey, not SaaS startup
- Think boutique engineering consultancy
- Trust through restraint and clarity
- Professional without being corporate
